About us

We are an Australian registered IT training school dedicated to providing students with high-quality information technology training. We adhere to the Australian Vocational Education and Training (VET) Framework (AQF) for education and training, and establish relevant education and training certification courses in accordance with the guidance of the Australian Skills and Quality Authority (ASQA) and the requirements of the Australian Government's Commonwealth Overseas Student International Curriculum Registry (CRICOS). Our courses focus on cultivating practical operational abilities and skills, ensuring that students can quickly integrate into the workplace after graduation.

Latest development

  • New course: We have recently launched a series of new courses to meet the constantly changing industry demands. These courses include popular fields such as advanced programming techniques, data analysis, and artificial intelligence. The new curriculum not only covers theoretical knowledge, but also emphasizes practical operations, aiming to cultivate students' ability to solve practical problems. We work closely with leading companies in the industry to ensure that course content is up-to-date and provide students with cutting-edge knowledge and skills.
  • cooperative project: We have established deep cooperative relationships with multiple well-known IT companies and jointly carried out a series of cooperative projects. These projects aim to provide students with internship and employment opportunities, while promoting knowledge sharing and technological exchange between schools and enterprises. By participating in collaborative projects, students can gain valuable practical experience, broaden their career horizons, and lay a solid foundation for their future career development.
  • scholarship and prize: We have established various scholarships and awards to motivate students to achieve excellent academic performance. Scholarships include academic achievement scholarships, innovation practice scholarships, etc., aimed at recognizing students who have outstanding academic and practical performance. In addition, we also collaborate with some organizations in the industry to provide students with additional scholarship opportunities. These scholarships not only help students reduce their financial burden, but also add a beautiful resume to their career development.
